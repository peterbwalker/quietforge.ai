What We Do

Quiet Forge Technologies builds agentic AI systems that automate complex, high-friction workflows in the mortgage industry. We focus on intelligent automation that operates across heterogeneous inputs and outputs—including voice, text, documents, and structured loan data.

Our systems go beyond traditional RPA and scripted chatbots. They are designed to understand intent, reason over context, and autonomously execute multi-step tasks, while maintaining appropriate human oversight.

Core Focus Areas

Multimodal Mortgage Automation

  • Voice → Text (automatic speech recognition)
  • Text → Voice (natural language speech synthesis)
  • Voice → Voice conversational automation

Agentic Workflow Orchestration

  • Goal-driven agents that plan and execute tasks autonomously
  • Context persistence across long-running borrower interactions
  • Human-in-the-loop escalation for compliance and edge cases

Mortgage Call Center Automation

  • Borrower intake and routing
  • Status checks and follow-ups
  • Document requests and validation
  • Servicing and post-close support workflows

Heterogeneous Data Integration

  • Calls, chat, email, and documents
  • Loan origination and servicing systems
  • Structured and unstructured data pipelines

Why Agentic AI

Mortgage operations are inherently multivariate, asynchronous, and fragmented across systems and teams. Agentic AI enables automation that can reason across these boundaries.

By shifting from rule-based automation to decision-oriented systems, lenders can reduce operational cost, improve cycle times, and deliver a more consistent borrower experience—without sacrificing control or compliance.
